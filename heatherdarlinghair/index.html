Heather Darling Hair

Heather was born and raised in Washington State but did all of her hair training with Paul Mitchell in Los Angeles before working in a Santa Monica Salon. Specializing in blondes she began working in San Francisco at E salon in 2015 and has honed her skill set in everything ranging from highlighting, Balayage, coloring, keratin treatment, and women’s hair cutting. Creating the best version of you is always her ultimate goal.
